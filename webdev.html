Portfolio Site: How I Created this Website

December 2023


I developed this portfolio for a MEDIART course, Web-Based Multimedia Communications. Throughout the course, we learned to code with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as prototyping in Figma. In addition, we learned the importance of developing accessible websites and critiqued website designs for their useability and accessibility. After finishing the course, I used my professor's critiques to continue developing my site.
